Why

Our Mission Statement

It is our desire to create a welcoming environment that fosters community. We believe that conversation is king, music is its queen and that haircuts allow us to do this. Cutting hair is NOT the most important thing we do. We want women to always feel welcome and never quite comfortable.

Each of us hope you have a favorite barber, and even better if they work here. Kids and Dogs are allowed to be kids and dogs. If that is a problem, then this is not the place for you. If it gets really busy, we will crank the volume of the music. If the music is too loud to speak, it means we are having a great day.
